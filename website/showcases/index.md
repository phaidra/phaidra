# Showcases { .phaidra-section-heading }

Discover how institutions across Europe are using PHAIDRA to solve real-world challenges in digital preservation, open access, and cultural heritage.

---

## [Belgrade University Library](belgrade.md)

**How the University of Belgrade Library Built a National Legacy on Open Source**

From securing the first digital copies of PhD theses to powering Serbia's national e-Science network and preserving migrant community stories, the University of Belgrade Library's fifteen-year journey with PHAIDRA shows how steady reliability becomes the ultimate feature.


---

## [Austrian Science Fund — FWF](fwf.md)

**Ensuring Meaningful Open Access for Publicly Funded Research**

When the FWF pioneered Open Access for monographs, digitally published books were being buried in publisher websites. By adopting PHAIDRA and integrating with OAPEN and OpenAIRE, the FWF transformed a policy commitment into genuine global academic visibility.


---

## [Vorarlberger Landesbibliothek](vorarlberger-landesbibliothek.md)

**A State Library with a Remit to Support Academia in a State with No University**

The Vorarlberger State Library took a unique headless approach to PHAIDRA, building its own Volare frontend while using PHAIDRA purely as a backend repository — enabling custom access controls, IIIF image delivery, crowdsourced metadata via Smapshot, and integration with Kulturpool and Europeana.
