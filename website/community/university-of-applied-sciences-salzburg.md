# University of Applied Sciences Salzburg

The Fachhochschule Salzburg GmbH is a university of applied sciences located in Puch bei Hallein, near Salzburg, Austria. It operates a PHAIDRA-based repository for publishing and sharing openly licensed teaching and learning materials (OER), and offers training programs to help educators create and publish their own open educational resources.
