# Office of the State Government of Lower Austria

DIRENO (Digitales Repositorium Niederösterreich) is a digital repository operated by the Office of the State Government of Lower Austria. It collects and permanently preserves digital assets related to Lower Austria, including academic publications, historical newspapers, and archival documents, in collaboration with various Lower Austrian state agencies and research organizations.
