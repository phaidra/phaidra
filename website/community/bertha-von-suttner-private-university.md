# Bertha von Suttner Private University

The Bertha von Suttner Privatuniversität St. Pölten GmbH is a private university in St. Pölten, Austria, specializing in psychology, psychotherapy, social work, and inclusion. It offers bachelor's, master's, and continuing education programs, with a strong focus on flexible online learning for working professionals.
